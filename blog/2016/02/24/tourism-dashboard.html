New Zealand Tourism Dashboard pushes Shiny to the max

24 Feb 2016

At a glance:

The New Zealand Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment has released a "Tourism Dashboard" built with R and Shiny to give flexible access to the full range of official statistics on tourism in New Zealand (disclaimer - I work for them).

Just a short note that in my day job we’ve released the New Zealand Tourism Dashboard, launched by the Associate Minister for Tourism earlier today. It’s built with R and Shiny and I won’t say more about it than that to avoid mixing up my work and outside-work hats. Except that it’s really really awesome, and there’s an enormous amount of data in there to explore. Great work by the team.
